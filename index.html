About the Celebrant

A brief profile—education, professional service, and a lifelong call to ministry.

Pastor Solomon Omoniyi Oluwagbemiga Ayeni

Chartered Accountant • Minister of God • Teacher of the Word
Honouring a Legacy

A Chartered Accountant and a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN), Pastor Ayeni attended Methodist High School, Ibadan (1976–1981), earned his OND/HND in Accountancy from The Polytechnic, Ibadan (Upper Credit, 1987), and obtained an MBA (Finance) from the University of Ibadan (2000).

His professional experience spans about 38 years across respected institutions including CRIN, PPFN, BSN, IITA, UNFPA, and ARFH—where he served as Director, Finance & Operations before retiring from secular work.

Beyond his profession, he has served God from his secondary school days—known for sound teaching, prayer, and practical leadership. He currently pastors The Apostolic Church Nigeria assembly at Afonta in Ibadan.

About the Book

God’s power for miracles, signs, and wonders still exists today—just as in the Bible days. Jesus Christ, the power of God, is the same yesterday, today, and forever.

POWER AS OF OLD invites readers to encounter God’s power in a fresh dimension, learn how to engage it for personal wellbeing, and experience deliverance from bondage—through faith in Christ.
